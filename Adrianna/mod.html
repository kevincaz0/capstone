Modern

Concluding classicism, Art Noveau was introduced to bring us into the era of Modern Architecture. Modern architecture completely altered the original styles of architecture in the most extreme ways. Discoveries still continue to be made today and ideas are built on by inspiring architects eveywhere. A far wider range of possibilities are created with this incredible change to architectural designs and expression.

Art Noveau

Art Noveau is an international style of art and architecture that includes designs inspired by the natural world using curved lines, organic shapes and arches. Characteristics of this style typically uses stained, curved glass, mosaics and asymmetrical shapes with a lot of detail. This style represents the nature of the world in a creative way. Out of this style came skyscrapers and other modernized designs intending to escape the usual structures from the past with a less restrictive art form.
