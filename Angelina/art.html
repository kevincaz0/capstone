Angelina Lynch

6.13.22

In the next four sections of the visual part of this website you will see how art has envolved. We will be able to take a look at the stoneage art, Ancient Egypts art work and then make our way into more modern art such as cartoons and street art. Its crazy to think and observe how art has changed so much throught hundreds of years, from finger paintings and carvings to detailed cartoons and expressing art through spraypainting.
