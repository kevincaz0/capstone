2000s Music: Pop

What is Pop?

Pop music is a genre of music that can theoretically be anything. Pop stands for popular so whatever is popular can be pop music. Currently the genre pop music is made up of music that can play on the radio. Catchy beats with lyrics that are easy to remember is the key to a radio song. Pop music is usually shorter music so people can listen to it easily. Pop is made up of guitars, drums, synths, and vocals are what makes a pop song.
