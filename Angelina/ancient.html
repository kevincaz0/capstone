Ancient Egypitan art

Ancient Egypt started around 3150 BC. We know this era by the artifacts we have from them. Mummified corpses such as king tut and their intresting artwork and hyroglifics that have been preserved for thousands of years. The Egyptians would use limestone when they would want to paint, using different colored pigments and a type of binder to have it stick on the stone. They also used stelite or Soapstone to make sculptures and used a technique called sunk relief.Most of the artwork was based on aspects of their lives for example the nile river, ancient gods,kingdoms,different animals and farming. Some artwork was even made for the gods to grand things such as immortality and success in life and death.
