1980s Music: Rock & Roll

What Is Rock & Roll?

Rock & Roll is a hard genre to describe. It has many variations plus sub genres that sort of completely change the sound. A blanket definition is music to dance to that's accompanied usually with guitars, drums and a strong vocalist. Rock & Roll is best to listen to loud which isn’t surprising considering that some of the biggest concerts ever are Rock & Roll. Rock & Roll. Just like a lot of music it derives and takes inspiration from other types. Rock & Roll derives from blues, jazz, and gospel.

Rock & Roll Artists

AC/DC

Ac/Dc are a rock band based in Sydney, Australia who were very popular in the 1980s. The active members are Angus Young, Phil Rudd, Cliff Williams, Brian Johnson, and Stevie Young. AC/DC are most well known for songs such as Back in Black and Thunderstruck. AC/DC were more popular with viewers than reviewers because AC/DC never changed their sound to fit in with popular trends. Which made loyal fans make AC/DC one of the most important bands in Rock music.

Queen

Queen are a rock band who are originally from London, United Kingdom who were well known for their songs Bohemian Rhapsody and Don’t Stop Me Now. The members' names are Freddie Mercury, Brain May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon. Many artists attribute the out of the box thinking to Queen.They were the first to not fit into stereotypes and genre. They were accepting of everyone, which is the main reason that people were attracted to them and caused them to be one of the biggest artists ever.

Kiss

Kiss are a rock band based in NYC, United States who were established in 1973 but grew in popularity in the 80s. The members' names are Paul Stanley, Gene Simmons, Ace Frehley, and Peter Crisis. Well known songs of Kiss are Psycho Circus and Jungle. Kiss were one of the first bands who performed in face paint. They put on a persona with makeup and they tried their best to never be seen without it. They impacted artists such as Slipknot and Daft punk. Every Time they’d perform the crowds became larger which shows their impact.

Bon Jovi

Bon Jovi are a rock band who are originally from Sayreville, New Jersey. The members of Bon Jovi consist of the lead singer Jon Bon Jovi, David Bryan, Tico Torres, Phil X, and Hugh McDonald. A few Bon Jovi songs that you may be able to recognize are Livin’ on a Prayer, Wanted Dead or Alive, and Runaway. Bon Jovi changed up their sound a few times from dance songs to more country. They laid the groundwork for the spread of rock music around the world.
