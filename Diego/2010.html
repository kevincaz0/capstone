2010s Music: Diversity

What is Diverse Music?

The type of music that made up the 2010s doesn’t fall into a certain genre like the other decades. Diverse music means Pop, Rap, R&B are all examples of popular music that took over the 10s. With the likes of Bruno Mars and Drake the 2010s had some big artists top the charts. The reason that the 10s are unlike other decades is because with the evolution of music genres have almost blended. Of course people can still be fully defined as rock, but Rap and R&B can coexist on the same song






Diverse Artists

Drake

Drake is a rap/hip hop artist that is from Toronto Canada. Drake's impact is huge on the music scene. He broke the seal for what makes a hip hop album. Drake sings and raps in his songs which separate artists from other artists at his time. Some of his most famous songs are One Dance and God's Plan. His most popular album is Views which had huge success on the charts which has some of his most popular songs on it.

Bruno Mars

Bruno Mars is an R&B singer who is originally from Honolulu Hawaii. He’s one of the best selling artists of all time whose music can be recognized around the world. Some of his most famous songs include Just The Way You Are and That’s What I Like. His most famous album is Doo-Wops and Hooligans. Bruno Mars has been nominated for 30 nominations and has won half of them. Since 2016 he has won every award he's been nominated for.

Taylor Swift

Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is a pop artist from Boca Raton Florida. Ariana Grande made her voice heard when she broke many spotify records with her first two albums Yours Truly and Me Everything. Ariana Grande has 6 #1 hits and has been in the top 10s 19 times. Some of her most famous songs are 7 rings and Thank U, Next. Ariana Grande has been nominated 15 times for a grammy and has won twice.
