1990s Music: Rap

What is Rap?

Rap music is a genre of music that originated from US black culture. 1990s rap can be classified as the golden age of hip hop because of the massive popularity that rap music created. During this time rap artists released projects and tracks that till this day are looked at for inspiration and a bar for present day artists.






Rap Artists

Tupac

Tupac is a west coast rapper whose impact left a huge mark on the rap scene. Tupac was the king of the west coast rapper scene. He was known for his songs How Do U Want It/ California Love, and Dear Mama. Tupac started the rapid growth of rap music. He’s attributed to be one of the most influential people in Hip Hop. His personal experience with racial discrimination related to what a lot of listeners went through. Which is why he was a massive part of rap in the 90s.

Jay Z

Jay Z was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York. He’s famous for his critically acclaimed albums that still to this day are argued to be some of the best albums of all time. Jay Z is known for his amazing writing and producing. Jay Z throughout the years showed different sides of him which is why people have related to him. Jay Z’s famous songs are Empire State of Mind and Numb/Encore. He made a massive impact that is still respected to this day.

Nas

Nas is a New York rapper whose albums are known to be one of the best in the game. Some famous songs from Nas include If I Ruled The World and N.Y. State of Mind. HIs most popular album Illmatic is one of the most influential albums of all time. Illmatic pushed the limits of rap music. With his fantastic lyrics, beats and production it made rappers push themselves to even try to be at the same level as this album.

Biggie Smalls

Biggie Smalls is an east coast rapper who was born in Brooklyn, New York. He was known for being one of the most influential rappers of all time right next to Tupac. He feuded with Tupac to see what coast was better: East coast or West coast. Biggie was known for his flow and how when he touched a mic the outcome was amazing. Some famous songs from Biggie are Warning, Suicidal Thoughts, and Hypnotize. His album Ready to Die is argued for best rap album of all time.
