THE STONE AGE ART

Looking into the era

The stone age was an era that occured 2.6 MILLION years ago! It was called the stoneage because in those times they would use stone tools and weapons to get around and even using it to light fires. Cavemen had 2 ways that they expressed there art, through carvings and painting cave walls with mud, dirt, and charcole sometimes mixed with animal fat. Most of the art was hand prints and people fighting animals with there spears. Other art included carvings and its believed during the stoneage they created art to pass time and self express or even help dealing with their stuggles.
