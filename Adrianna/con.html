Contemporary

When you see buildings with unusual designs and structures, you're looking at contemporary architecture. Contemporary architecture isn't exactly a specific design or style but it is rather the work of our 21st century architectures collectively using all types of styles to make completely crazy and seemingly unrealistic buildings. With the use of traditional architecture, postmodernism and high-tech architecture, buildings are created using free-form shapes, carefree of proportions and symmetry. Contemporary architecture allows us to get away from typical designs to create something that no one has seen before. With technological advancements and total openmindness when it comes to creativity, we are able to introduce a new era to architecture.
