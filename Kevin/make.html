What Is Makeup?

Makeup is products used to enhance the beauty of a person’s appearance , females used it but in modern time , anyone can use makeup.

How Make Up Started

The earliest historical record of makeup is from the 1st Dynasty of Egypt (c. 3100-2907 BC). It was first used to appeal to the gods but later over time women from other places would use it to make themselves look like the beauty standards at the time. Which was trying to make their skin look pale and some would make their eyeshadow cool tones.

How Make Up Evolved

Over time the beauty standards changed and it being more alive , women would start wearing tan colors and they would add a lot of blush , which was in the 2000’s. Eventually they started creating shades for people and making multiple colors, and men started using makeup. Now that men started using makeup , it would create other things like drag queens.
